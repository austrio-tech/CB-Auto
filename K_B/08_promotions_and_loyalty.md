## Loyalty Program — Ali Points

### How It Works

Every purchase earns **Ali Points**. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Earning Rate** | OMR 1 spent = 10 Ali Points |
| **Redemption Rate** | 100 Ali Points = OMR 0.100 discount (10% back) |
| **Minimum Redemption** | 500 Ali Points = OMR 0.500 per order |
| **Maximum per Order** | Up to 30% of any single order's value |
| **Points Expiry** | 12 months after earning (reset by any purchase) |

### Tier Levels

| Tier | Points Required | Benefits |
|------|----------------|---------|
| **Silver** | 0 – 2,999 points | Standard earning rate |
| **Gold** | 3,000 – 9,999 points | 1.25x earning multiplier, priority support |
| **Platinum** | 10,000+ points | 1.5x earning multiplier, pre-launch product access, dedicated account manager |

### Bonus Points Events

- **Birthday month:** Double points (20 per OMR 1 spent) during your birth month.
- **New product launch:** Triple points for 48 hours on newly added products.
- **Referral:** 500 bonus points when a friend creates an account using your referral code.

### How to View Your Points

Go to **Account Settings** in the app. Your current Ali Points balance is shown on the profile screen.

## Seasonal Buying Guide for Oman

| Period | Recommended Actions |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Jan–Mar** (Winter) | Check/replace wipers; full car service; air filter and oil change |
| **Apr–May** (Pre-Summer) | Battery check; coolant flush; stock up on engine oil |
| **Jun–Aug** (Peak Summer) | Batteries, coolant, radiator hoses; cabin air filter; tyre inspection |
| **Jul–Sep** (Khareef — Salalah) | Wiper replacement essential; brake check for wet roads; lights/electrical |
| **Oct–Nov** (Pre-National Day) | General service; shock absorber inspection; belts and hoses check |
| **December** (Winter begins) | Coolant check (mountain areas); windscreen washer fluid |
