## Maintenance Interval Reference (Oman Climate)

All intervals are adjusted for Oman's hot, dusty environment. Follow whichever comes first (km or time).

| Service Item | Oman Interval | European Interval |
|-------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Engine Oil (Conventional) | 5,000 km / 3 months | 10,000 km / 12 months |
| Engine Oil (Full Synthetic) | 8,000 km / 6 months | 15,000 km / 12 months |
| Oil Filter | Same as oil change | Same as oil change |
| Air Filter Inspection | 10,000 km | 20,000 km |
| Air Filter Replacement | 15,000 km | 40,000 km |
| Cabin Air Filter | 15,000 km | 25,000 km |
| Fuel Filter | 30,000 km | 50,000–80,000 km |
| Spark Plugs (Copper) | 20,000 km | 30,000 km |
| Spark Plugs (Iridium/Platinum) | 40,000 km | 60,000–100,000 km |
| Brake Pads Inspection | 15,000 km | 25,000 km |
| Brake Fluid | 2 years / 40,000 km | 2 years / 40,000 km |
| Coolant Flush | 2 years / 40,000 km | 5 years / 100,000 km |
| Battery Inspection | Every 12 months | Every 2 years |
| Battery Replacement | 2–3 years | 4–5 years |
| Wiper Blades | 12–18 months | 2 years |
| Transmission Fluid (Auto) | 40,000 km | 60,000 km |
| Power Steering Fluid | 40,000 km | Lifetime (in some cars) |
| Shock Absorbers Inspection | 30,000 km | 50,000 km |

## Part Number Lookup

If you have a specific OEM part number:
1. Search for it directly in the app's search bar.
2. Or contact any branch with the part number and we will check stock availability.
3. You can also WhatsApp the part number to **+968 9576 0754** for a quick price check.

If you have a **VIN (Vehicle Identification Number):**  
Send it via WhatsApp and our team can look up the exact OEM replacement parts for your specific vehicle.

VIN location: Usually on the dashboard (visible through the windscreen), inside the driver's door jamb, or on the vehicle registration document.
