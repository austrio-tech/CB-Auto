## Section A — Account & Registration

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: Open the Ali Grandsons app and tap **CREATE ACCOUNT** on the home screen. Fill in your username, full name, email address, phone number, password (minimum 8 characters), and date of birth. Tap CREATE ACCOUNT to submit. You will be redirected to the login screen.

**Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?**  
A: Contact us via WhatsApp at +968 9576 0754 or email aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com with your registered email address and we will reset your password and send you a new one by email.

**Q: Can I change my username after registration?**  
A: No. Your username is permanent and cannot be changed after registration. You may update all other profile details (name, email, phone, password) from Account Settings (side drawer → Account Settings).

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?**  
A: No. Each email address can only be linked to one account.

**Q: How do I update my profile information?**  
A: Open the side drawer, select Account Settings, edit the relevant fields, and tap SAVE CHANGES.

**Q: How do I change my password?**  
A: Go to Account Settings (side drawer → Account Settings), update the password field, and tap SAVE CHANGES.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: To delete your account, contact our support team at aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All order history will be lost.

**Q: I cannot log in — the app says invalid credentials.**  
A: Make sure you are using your **email address** (not username) to log in. Check that Caps Lock is not on for your password. Ensure you have a working internet connection. If the problem persists, contact support to verify your account status.

## Section B — Browsing & Searching Products

**Q: How do I search for a product?**  
A: Use the search bar at the top of the dashboard. Type a product name (e.g. "brake pad"), a brand (e.g. "Bosch"), or a car model (e.g. "Land Cruiser"). Results update as you type.

**Q: How do I know if a part fits my car?**  
A: Each product listing shows the Type (vehicle category), Brand, and Compatibility/Model field. If you are unsure, contact us on WhatsApp (+968 9576 0754) with your car's make, model, year, and VIN number.

**Q: A product I need is shown as "Out of Stock". What do I do?**  
A: Options: (1) Check back in the app — stock is updated in real time. (2) Contact a branch directly. (3) You will receive an **automatic email notification** when the item is back in stock.

**Q: Can I see the product image before buying?**  
A: Yes. Each product has a photo viewable on the product detail page. Tap any product card to see the full image.

**Q: Does the price include VAT or other taxes?**  
A: Yes. All displayed prices are final prices including all applicable taxes. There are no hidden fees.

## Section C — Cart & Checkout

**Q: How do I add a product to my cart?**  
A: Open a product's detail page, tap ADD TO CART, enter the quantity you want, and tap ADD. The item is added to your cart instantly.

**Q: How do I view my cart?**  
A: Tap the shopping bag icon in the top-right corner of the dashboard. A gold badge shows how many different items are in your cart.

**Q: How do I remove an item from my cart?**  
A: In the cart screen, tap the red trash icon next to the item you want to remove.

**Q: How do I change the quantity of an item in my cart?**  
A: In the cart screen, use the + and − buttons next to each item. You cannot increase the quantity beyond what is in stock.

**Q: My cart is empty after I logged back in. What happened?**  
A: Your cart is linked to your account and should persist between sessions. If it appears empty, the items may have sold out or been removed. Please re-add them from the catalogue.

**Q: Is there a maximum number of items I can have in my cart?**  
A: There is no hard limit on the number of different products. For quantity, you are limited by available stock.

**Q: Can I save items for later (wishlist)?**  
A: A wishlist feature is not available in the current version. You can keep items in your cart — they will remain until you checkout or remove them.

## Section D — Orders

**Q: How do I place an order?**  
A: Browse the catalogue, add items to your cart, tap PROCEED TO CHECKOUT, fill in your delivery address and payment details, then tap CONFIRM & PLACE ORDER.

**Q: How do I track my order?**  
A: Open the side drawer and tap **My Orders**. The ACTIVE tab shows all current orders with real-time status (Pending → Ready → In Delivery → Delivered). You also receive email notifications for every status change.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**  
A: You can request cancellation while the order status is **PENDING** by contacting us via WhatsApp (+968 9576 0754) or email (aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com). Orders that are READY or IN DELIVERY cannot be cancelled.

**Q: I placed an order but have not received a confirmation. What should I do?**  
A: Check your email inbox (including spam/junk folder) for a confirmation email from aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com. Also check the My Orders section in the app. If you still cannot find your order after 30 minutes, contact support.

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**  
A: Orders cannot be modified after being placed. Cancel the order (if still in PENDING status) and place a new one with the correct items.

**Q: What does the order number look like?**  
A: Order numbers are formatted as #00001 through #99999 (5-digit format). You will see it in the My Orders screen and in all email notifications.

**Q: How do I view the items in an old order?**  
A: Go to My Orders, tap the COMPLETED tab, then tap any past order to see the full detail including all items, quantities, prices, and delivery information.

A: Delivery times vary by location:

|------|----------------|
| Muscat | Same day (orders before 2 PM) or next business day |
| Salalah | 1–2 business days |
| Sohar | Same day or next day |
| Nizwa | Next business day |
| Sur | 1–2 business days |
| Buraimi | Same day or next day |
| Ibri | Next business day |
| Remote areas | 3–5 business days |

**Q: How much does delivery cost?**  
A: Delivery is completely **FREE** on all orders. There is no minimum order amount.

**Q: Can I pick up my order from a branch instead of delivery?**  
A: Yes. Select Self Pick-Up during checkout and choose your preferred branch. You will receive a notification when the order is ready for collection (usually 2–4 hours during business hours).

**Q: What if I am not home when the delivery arrives?**  
A: The driver will call your registered phone number. If you are unreachable, the order is returned to the branch and a new delivery will be scheduled.

**Q: Do you deliver on Fridays?**  
A: Limited Friday deliveries are available in Muscat from 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM only. All other governorates: no Friday deliveries.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?**  
A: Yes, while the order is PENDING. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp with your order number and the new address.

## Section F — Payment

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: We accept:
1. Cash on Delivery (COD) — pay when the order arrives
2. Credit/Debit Card — Visa, Mastercard, Oman Net, AmEx (in-app)
3. Bank Transfer — for wholesale/corporate accounts only
4. Branch payment — cash or card when collecting at a branch

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes. Card payments are processed through a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store your full card number on our systems.

**Q: Can I pay part cash and part card?**  
A: No. Each order must use a single payment method.

**Q: My card was charged but the order did not go through. What happens?**  
A: If the app shows an error after a card payment, the charge is usually a temporary hold that will be automatically reversed within 3–5 business days. Contact your bank or our support team if you need confirmation.

## Section H — Technical Vehicle Questions

**Q: How often should I change my engine oil filter in Oman?**  
A: More frequently than European recommendations. In Oman's dusty climate, change your oil filter every **5,000 km** with conventional oil, or every **8,000–10,000 km** with full synthetic oil.

**Q: My car battery keeps dying in summer. Why?**  
A: Extreme heat (40–50°C in Oman's summer) accelerates battery degradation. Batteries typically last only **2–3 years** in Oman. Have your battery tested **free of charge** at any of our branches.

**Q: How often should I change my wiper blades?**  
A: Every 12–18 months in normal use. In Salalah during the Khareef (monsoon), replace them before July. Desert sand can also degrade wiper rubber faster than rain alone.

**Q: My air conditioner smells bad. What part do I need?**  
A: You likely need a new **Cabin Air Filter** (also called Pollen Filter). Replace every 15,000–20,000 km.

**Q: Which brake pads should I choose — ceramic or semi-metallic?**  
A: For everyday city driving in Muscat: **Ceramic brake pads** (Brembo) — quieter, cleaner, less dust. For heavy SUVs, off-road use (Nizwa, Buraimi, Dhofar): **Semi-metallic** (Bosch) — better heat resistance.
