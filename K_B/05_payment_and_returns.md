## Refunds Policy

### Eligibility for Refund or Exchange

You are eligible for a **full refund or free exchange** if:

1. The wrong item was delivered (different from what you ordered).
2. The item arrived damaged or defective.
3. The item is covered by a warranty claim (defect within warranty period).
4. The order was cancelled before dispatch (COD orders only; card payments are refunded).

### Items NOT Eligible for Refund or Exchange

- Consumable items (filters, wiper blades, spark plugs) once installed.
- Items damaged due to incorrect installation by the customer.
- Items returned without original packaging after 14 days.
- Items that show signs of use, modification, or tampering.
- Special-order items (parts ordered specifically for a customer not in standard stock).

### Refund Timeframes

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|----------------|----------------|
| Cash on Delivery | Store credit (immediate) OR cash at branch within 5 business days |
| Card | Credited back to original card within 5–7 business days |
| Bank Transfer (wholesale) | Processed within 7–10 business days |

## Return Process

### How to Initiate a Return

**Step 1:** Contact our support team within the return window:
- Damaged / Wrong item: within **48 hours** of delivery
- Warranty claim: within the warranty period
- Change of mind: within **7 days** (item unused, in original packaging)

Contact via: WhatsApp (+968 9576 0754), Email (aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com), or visit any branch.

**Step 2:** Provide the following:
- Order Number (e.g. #00042)
- Product name and reason for return
- Photos of the item and packaging (for damaged/wrong items)

**Step 3:** We will review your request within 1 business day and confirm whether the return is approved and how to send the item back.

**Step 4:** Return the item in its original packaging with all included accessories.

**Step 5:** Once received and inspected, your refund or replacement will be processed.

## Warranty Claims

### How to Claim Warranty

1. Contact support with your order number and a description of the defect.
2. Our technical team may ask for photos or a brief diagnostic report.
3. For brake parts and suspension components, the claim may require inspection at a partner workshop.
4. Approved claims are replaced free of charge (including delivery of the replacement).

### Warranty Is Void If

- The part was installed by an unqualified person (not a certified mechanic).
- The vehicle was used in racing or off-road competitions.
- The part was modified in any way.
- The damage is caused by external factors (accident, flood, fire).

## Frequently Asked Questions — Payment & Returns

**Q: Is my card information safe when I pay in the app?**  
A: Yes. We use an encrypted payment gateway and do not store your full card number. Your security is our priority.

**Q: Can I pay part cash and part card?**  
A: No. Each order must be paid with a single payment method.

**Q: I paid by card but my order was cancelled. When will I get my refund?**  
A: Your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original card. You will receive a confirmation email.

**Q: I received a damaged item. Do I need to pay for return shipping?**  
A: No. For damaged, defective, or wrong items, we cover all return logistics costs.

**Q: Can I exchange a part for a different size or brand?**  
A: Yes. Contact support, return the original unused item, and we will apply the credit to a new order.

**Q: What currency can I pay in?**  
A: Omani Rials (OMR) only for Cash on Delivery and in-store purchases. Cards may be charged in OMR depending on your card's settings.

**Q: Do you offer installment payment plans?**  
A: Not at this time. All orders must be paid in full at checkout or on delivery.

**Q: I changed my mind about an order. Can I return it?**  
A: Yes, within 7 days of delivery, if the item is completely unused and in its original packaging. A 10% restocking fee may apply on change-of-mind returns for orders above OMR 50.
