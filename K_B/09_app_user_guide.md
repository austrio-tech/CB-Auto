# Knowledge Base — Document 09: App User Guide (Step-by-Step Instructions)

This document explains every screen and feature in the Ali Grandsons mobile app from a user's perspective.

---

## Getting Started

### Installing the App

- Search **"Ali Grandsons"** on Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS).
- Download and install the app (free download, ~25 MB).
- Open the app after installation.

### First Launch

When the app starts for the first time you will see the **Home screen** showing:
- The Ali Grandson logo in the centre
- "ALI GRANDSONS" and "Premium Spare Parts" text
- Three buttons: **CUSTOMER LOGIN**, **CREATE ACCOUNT**, **ADMIN ACCESS**

If you have already logged in previously, the app will take you directly to your dashboard without showing the home screen.

---

## Creating a New Customer Account

1. Tap **CREATE ACCOUNT** on the home screen.
2. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| Username | A unique ID you choose (e.g. ahmed_92). **Cannot be changed later.** |
| Full Name | Your real first and last name |
| Email Address | A valid email you check regularly (used for login and notifications) |
| Phone Number | Your Omani mobile number (e.g. +968 9123 4567) |
| Password | At least 8 characters — make it strong |
| Date of Birth | Tap the field and pick your birth date from the calendar |

3. Tap **CREATE ACCOUNT**.
4. If the username or email is already taken, the app will tell you — choose a different one.
5. On success, you will be taken to the Login screen with a "Account created!" message.
6. Log in with your email and password.

---

## Logging In

### Customer Login
1. Tap **CUSTOMER LOGIN** on the home screen.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Tap **SIGN IN**.
4. If credentials are correct, you go directly to the Customer Dashboard.
5. If incorrect, an "Invalid email or password" message appears — check spelling and try again.

### Admin Login
1. Tap **ADMIN ACCESS** (small text at the bottom of the home screen).
2. Enter the Admin ID and password.
3. Tap **AUTHENTICATE**.
4. Default admin credentials: ID = `admin`, Password = `admin123`

### Staying Logged In
The app remembers your login. When you close and reopen the app, you will be taken straight to your dashboard — you do not need to log in every time.

## Customer Dashboard (Main Screen)

### Top Bar
- "ALI GRANDSONS" title in the centre
- Shopping bag icon (top right) — shows a **gold badge** with cart item count

### Side Drawer
Swipe from the left edge OR tap the menu icon to open the side drawer:
- Browse Catalog (current screen)
- My Orders
- Account Settings
- Help & Support
- Sign Out

### Banner Carousel
Three promotional banner images that automatically slide every 5 seconds. Swipe left or right manually. Small dots below show which banner is active.

### Search Bar
Type any word to search the product catalogue. Results update as you type.  
Examples: "brake", "NGK", "Corolla", "filter", "Bosch"  
Clear the search by tapping the **X** in the search bar.

### Product List
Each product card shows:
- Product image (left side)
- Brand name (in gold, small text above the product name)
- Product name (large bold text)
- Short description (up to 2 lines)
- Price in OMR (left)
- **IN STOCK** / **OUT OF STOCK** badge (right, green or red)

Tap any product card to view its full detail page.

> Pull down on the product list to refresh the catalogue with the latest stock information.

---

## Product Detail Page

Tapping a product card opens a full-screen detail view with:
- Large product image at the top (expandable hero image)
- Brand name (in gold)
- Product name and price in OMR
- In Stock / Out of Stock indicator showing exact quantity available
- Full product description and specifications

### Adding to Cart
- If In Stock: tap the **"ADD TO CART"** button at the bottom.
- A dialog asks "How many units would you like to add?"
- Enter a number (cannot exceed available stock).
- Tap **ADD**. The item is added and you are returned to the catalogue.
- If Out of Stock: the button is greyed out and says **"OUT OF STOCK"**.

---

## Shopping Cart

### Access Your Cart
Tap the **bag icon** in the top-right of the dashboard.

### What You See in the Cart
- Each item shows: product image, name, price, and quantity
- **+/-** buttons to change quantity
- **Trash icon** to remove the item
- A running total at the bottom

> The + button will not let you exceed the available stock. If you try, you see: "Only X items available in stock."

### Checkout Button
At the bottom: **"PROCEED TO CHECKOUT"** button and the total amount in OMR.

---

## Checkout / Placing an Order

### Delivery Information Section
Fill in:
1. Full Delivery Address — be specific: building number, street, area, city
2. Contact Phone Number — pre-filled from your profile
3. Special Instructions — optional (e.g. "Ground floor, Building B", "Call 30 minutes before")

### Payment Method Section
Choose one:
- **Cash on Delivery** — no extra action needed
- **Card** — enter card holder name, card number, expiry date (MM/YY), and CVV

### Order Summary Section
Shows: Items Total, Delivery Fee: **FREE**, Grand Total (in gold text).

### Placing the Order
Tap **CONFIRM & PLACE ORDER**. Wait briefly while the order is processed. You will see a success message and be taken back to the dashboard. Check My Orders for your new order.

## My Orders Screen

**Access:** Side drawer → My Orders

### Two Tabs

**ACTIVE TAB** — Orders not yet completed:
- Pending (yellow badge)
- Ready (blue badge)
- In Delivery (gold badge)

**COMPLETED TAB** — Finalised orders:
- Delivered (green badge)
- Cancelled (red badge)

### Viewing Order Details
Tap any order card to open the full detail view showing:
- Status banner (colour-coded)
- Order summary (date, payment mode, total, completion date)
- Delivery details (address, phone, special notes)
- List of all items ordered (name, quantity, unit price)

---

## Account Settings (Profile)

**Access:** Side drawer → Account Settings

### What You Can Change
- Full Name
- Email Address (checked for uniqueness)
- Phone Number
- Password (minimum 8 characters)

### What You Cannot Change
- Username (permanent after registration)

Fill in the new values and tap **SAVE CHANGES**. A green success message confirms the update.

---

## Help & Support (FAQ Chatbot)

**Access:** Side drawer → Help & Support

### How It Works
1. The screen opens with a greeting from the Ali Grandson assistant.
2. Tap the **"ASK A QUESTION"** floating button at the bottom.
3. A list of common questions (FAQs) appears in a popup sheet.
4. Tap any question to see the answer.
5. The question and answer appear as chat bubbles — your question on the right (maroon), the answer on the left (white).
6. You can ask multiple questions in one session.

For questions not in the FAQ list, contact support directly.

---

## Sign Out

1. Open the side drawer (swipe from left or tap menu icon).
2. Scroll to the bottom of the drawer.
3. Tap **Sign Out** (shown in red).
4. You are logged out and returned to the Home screen.

The app will not remember your session after signing out. You will need to log in again next time.

## Troubleshooting Common App Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|---------|
| App not loading / blank screen | Check internet connection. Close and reopen the app. If persistent, reinstall. |
| Cannot log in | Make sure you are using your **email** (not username) to log in. Check Caps Lock. |
| Product images not loading | Usually a slow internet connection — wait a few seconds or refresh. |
| Cart items disappeared | Check the cart again after logging back in. Items may have sold out. |
| Order not appearing in My Orders | Pull down to refresh. Wait up to 2 minutes after placing an order. |
| Notification email not received | Check spam/junk folder. Verify the email address in Account Settings. |
| App is running slowly | Close other apps. Ensure at least 100 MB of free storage. Update the app. |
