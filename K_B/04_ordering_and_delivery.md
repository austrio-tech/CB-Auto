## How to Place an Order (App)

### Step 1 — Create or Log In to Your Account
- Open the Ali Grandsons app on your phone.
- If you are a new customer, tap **CREATE ACCOUNT** and fill in your details.
- If you already have an account, tap **CUSTOMER LOGIN** and enter your email and password.

### Step 2 — Browse the Product Catalogue
- On the dashboard, you will see the product catalogue below the promotional banners.
- Use the search bar to search by product name, brand, or car model.
- Example searches: "brake pads", "Bosch", "Land Cruiser", "oil filter"

### Step 3 — View a Product
- Tap any product card to open the full product detail page.
- You will see: product name and brand, full description, vehicle compatibility, price in OMR, and In Stock / Out of Stock status.

### Step 4 — Add to Cart
- Tap **ADD TO CART** at the bottom of the product page.
- A dialog asks "How many units would you like to add?"
- Enter a quantity (the app will not allow you to order more than what is in stock).
- Tap **ADD** to confirm. The cart badge in the top-right corner updates.

### Step 5 — Review Your Cart
- Tap the bag icon to open your Shopping Cart.
- Here you can increase or decrease quantities, remove items, and see a live-updated total.
- Quantities cannot exceed available stock.

### Step 6 — Proceed to Checkout
- Tap **PROCEED TO CHECKOUT**.
- Fill in your **Delivery Information**: full address, contact phone number, and optional special instructions.
- Choose your **Payment Method**: Cash on Delivery (COD) or Card.

### Step 7 — Confirm and Place Order
- Review the Order Summary (Items Total, Delivery Fee: **FREE**, Grand Total).
- Tap **CONFIRM & PLACE ORDER**.
- Your order is submitted instantly and the admin receives an email notification.

### Step 8 — Track Your Order
- Go to **My Orders** (via the side drawer).
- The **ACTIVE** tab shows your current orders with live status.

## Order Status Descriptions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Order received, staff are reviewing and preparing. Expected: 1–4 hours during business hours. |
| **Ready** | Order packed and ready to dispatch. If Self Pick-Up, you can collect now. |
| **In Delivery** | Order left the warehouse and is on its way to you. |
| **Delivered** | Successfully delivered. Final status for a completed order. |
| **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled (reason always provided by email). |

## Delivery Policy

### Delivery Fees

- **FREE** on all orders, regardless of order size or delivery location.
- No minimum order value required.

### Delivery Timeframes

| Area | Estimated Delivery |
|------|--------------------|
| Muscat Governorate | Same day (orders before 2:00 PM) / Next business day |
| Salalah / Dhofar | 1 – 2 Business Days |
| Sohar / North Batinah | Same Day / Next Day |
| Sur / South Sharqiyah | 1 – 2 Business Days |
| Nizwa / Ad Dakhiliyah | Next Business Day |
| Buraimi / Al Buraimi | Same Day / Next Day |
| Ibri / Ad Dhahirah | Next Business Day |
| Remote / Interior Areas | 3 – 5 Business Days |

> "Business days" means Saturday through Thursday. Friday is a partial working day (morning only + evening).

### Delivery Hours
Deliveries are made between **9:00 AM and 8:00 PM**, Saturday to Thursday. No Friday morning deliveries.

### Delivery Tracking
Track your order in the **MY ORDERS** section of the app at any time. Email notifications are sent when the status changes.

### Proof of Delivery
The delivery person will ask you to confirm receipt. For COD orders, payment is collected at the door.

## Cancellation Policy

- You can request cancellation while the order is in **PENDING** status.
- Contact support via WhatsApp (+968 9576 0754) or email (aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com) with your Order Number.
- Orders in **READY** or **IN DELIVERY** status cannot be cancelled.
- Once **DELIVERED**, the standard returns policy applies (see Document 05).

**Admin-Initiated Cancellations:** If we cancel your order (e.g. due to stock discrepancy), you will receive an email with a clear reason. No charge will be made.

Contact us within 48 hours. We will ship the missing item immediately at no charge.

## Frequently Asked Questions — Ordering & Delivery

**Q: Can I order from the app even if I am not near a branch?**  
A: Yes. The app accepts orders from anywhere in Oman. We deliver nationwide.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?**  
A: Yes, while the order is in PENDING status. Contact us via WhatsApp immediately.

**Q: What happens if I am not home when the delivery arrives?**  
A: The delivery driver will attempt to call the number on your order. If unreachable, the order will be returned to the branch and a new delivery will be scheduled.

**Q: Do you deliver to PO Boxes?**  
A: No. We require a physical street address for delivery.

**Q: Can I place an order for someone else (as a gift)?**  
A: Yes. Just enter the recipient's address and phone number in the delivery information fields.

**Q: Is there a limit on how many items I can order?**  
A: For retail customers, there is no specific limit. For very large orders (e.g. 50+ units of one item), please contact the branch directly to confirm stock availability.

**Q: How do I know if a product is available in my city?**  
A: The product listing shows overall stock. If ordering from a regional branch, availability is confirmed before dispatch. You will be notified if there is any delay.
