## Ordering

**[INTENT: How to place an order]**  
Placing an order is easy! Here are the steps:
1. Log in to the app.
2. Browse the catalogue or search for your part.
3. Tap a product and tap ADD TO CART.
4. When ready, tap the bag icon and then PROCEED TO CHECKOUT.
5. Enter your delivery address and choose payment method (Cash on Delivery or Card).
6. Tap CONFIRM & PLACE ORDER. Done!

You will get email notifications as your order progresses.

**[INTENT: Can I order without account]**  
No, you need a free account to order. Registration takes under 2 minutes — tap CREATE ACCOUNT on the home screen and fill in your details.

**[INTENT: Minimum order]**  
There is no minimum order value. You can order a single item and receive it with free delivery anywhere in Oman!

**[INTENT: Order confirmation / where is my confirmation]**  
After placing an order, check your email inbox (including spam/junk) for a confirmation from aligrandsoncompany@gmail.com. You can also view your order in the app under My Orders (side drawer → My Orders).

## Products

**[INTENT: Do you have / availability check]**  
To check if we have a specific part, search for it in the app using the search bar. If it shows **IN STOCK**, you can order immediately. If it shows **OUT OF STOCK**, you will be emailed automatically when it is available. You can also WhatsApp us at +968 9576 0754 to check stock across all branches.

**[INTENT: Part for specific vehicle]**  
To find the right part for your vehicle, it helps to know:
- Car make (e.g. Toyota, Nissan)
- Car model (e.g. Land Cruiser, Patrol)
- Year (e.g. 2019)
- Engine type (e.g. 4.0L petrol, 2.8L diesel)
- What part you need (e.g. brake pads, air filter)

Search in the app or WhatsApp us the details and we will confirm the right part number and price for you.

**[INTENT: Brake pads]**  
We stock brake pads from **Brembo** (Italy) and **Bosch** (Germany) for all vehicle types. Brembo ceramic pads are ideal for sedans and everyday driving (quieter, less dust). Bosch semi-metallic pads are better for heavy SUVs and off-road use (higher heat resistance). Prices range from OMR 11 to OMR 40 depending on vehicle and compound type. Search "brake pad" in the app to see all options.

**[INTENT: Oil filter]**  
We stock **Bosch** oil filters for most vehicles — one of the most trusted filter brands. Prices range from OMR 4 to OMR 9. In Oman's dusty climate, change your oil filter every 5,000 km with conventional oil or every 8,000–10,000 km with synthetic oil. Search "oil filter" in the app.

**[INTENT: Spark plugs]**  
We stock **NGK iridium spark plugs** — the world's leading brand. Iridium plugs give better fuel efficiency and last 40,000–60,000 km. Prices are OMR 2.80–OMR 8 per plug (replace as a full set). Search "spark plug" in the app.

**[INTENT: Air filter]**  
We stock **K&N high-flow air filters** — washable and reusable (last up to 50,000 km). In Oman's dusty environment, inspect your air filter every 10,000 km and replace by 15,000–20,000 km. Prices range OMR 8.50–OMR 22. Search "air filter" in the app.

**[INTENT: Wiper blades]**  
**Rain-X wiper blades** are our top seller — they apply a water-repellent coating as they wipe. Available in sizes for most vehicles. Prices OMR 7–OMR 16 per pair. If you are in Salalah, replace your wipers before the Khareef (monsoon) season in July. Search "wiper" in the app.

**[INTENT: Battery]**  
We stock **Varta AGM** and standard batteries. Batteries in Oman typically last 2–3 years due to extreme heat. **FREE battery testing** is available at all 7 branches — just come in! Prices range OMR 24–OMR 65. Search "battery" in the app.

**[INTENT: Shock absorbers]**  
We stock **KYB** and **Monroe** shock absorbers for all vehicle types. Prices: OMR 28–OMR 70 per unit. For off-road driving (common in Oman), upgraded shocks are recommended over standard OEM replacements. Inspect every 30,000 km. WhatsApp us your car model for the exact part.
