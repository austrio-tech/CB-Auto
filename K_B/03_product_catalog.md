## Ordering by Vehicle Compatibility

To find the right part for your vehicle, provide the following when using the app or contacting support:

1. **Vehicle Make** (e.g. Toyota, Nissan, Mitsubishi)
2. **Vehicle Model** (e.g. Land Cruiser, Patrol, Pajero)
3. **Year of Manufacture** (e.g. 2019)
4. **Engine Size / Type** (e.g. 4.0L V6 Petrol, 2.5L Diesel)
5. **Transmission Type** (Auto / Manual)
6. **Part Required** (e.g. Front brake pads, air filter)
7. **VIN** (optional but preferred for exact fitment)

## Product Condition Policy

| Condition | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **New Genuine (OEM)** | Factory-original parts. Full warranty. Highest price. |
| **New Aftermarket** | Quality-equivalent parts from certified brands. Warranty varies. |
| **Exchange / Rebuilt** | Remanufactured alternators, starters, turbos. Limited warranty. Customer must return the old core part. |
| **Second-Hand / Used** | **NOT SOLD.** Ali Grandson only sells new or remanufactured parts. |
