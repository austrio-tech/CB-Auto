## About the Company

Ali Grandson Spare Parts has been serving the Omani automotive market for over two decades. Starting as a small family-run shop in Muscat's Industrial Area, the company has grown into one of the most trusted spare parts suppliers in the Sultanate, with branches and distribution warehouses across six major cities.

The company specialises in supplying genuine OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts as well as high-quality aftermarket alternatives. All products are sourced from internationally certified suppliers including **Brembo** (Italy), **Bosch** (Germany), **NGK** (Japan), **K&N** (USA), and **Rain-X** (USA), among others.

## Frequently Asked Questions — About the Company

**Q: Is Ali Grandson Spare Parts an official dealer?**  
A: Yes. We are an authorised reseller for several international brands including Bosch, NGK, K&N, and Rain-X. For Brembo products, we are a certified distributor for the Muscat and Dhofar governorates.

**Q: How long has the company been operating?**  
A: Since 2025 — with over a year of experience in the Omani automotive market.

**Q: Does the company sell parts for all car brands?**  
A: We cover most popular brands sold in Oman including Toyota, Nissan, Mitsubishi, Hyundai, Kia, Ford, Chevrolet, Land Rover, and more. Contact us if your vehicle's make/model is not listed.

**Q: Can I visit the store to pick up my order?**  
A: Yes. You can select "Self Pick-Up" at checkout and collect your order from your nearest branch. See Document 02 for all branch locations and addresses.

**Q: Does the company export parts to other GCC countries?**  
A: At this time, we serve customers within the Sultanate of Oman only. GCC export services are under development.
