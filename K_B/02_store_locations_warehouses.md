## Self Pick-Up Instructions

1. Place your order via the app and select **"Self Pick-Up"** as the delivery method.
2. Choose your preferred branch from the list.
3. You will receive an app notification and an email when the order is ready (usually within 2–4 hours during business hours).
4. Visit the branch, show the order confirmation number on your app (Order #XXXXX), and collect your items.
5. Bring a valid Omani ID (civil card) or a copy of the order email for verification.
